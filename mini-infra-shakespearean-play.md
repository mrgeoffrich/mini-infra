# The Tragedy of Mini Infra
## A Shakespearean Play in Five Acts

*Dramatis Personae:*

- **DOCKERUS**, Lord of Containers, Duke of Virtualization
- **REACTIA**, Princess of the Frontend Realm
- **EXPRESSIO**, Noble Knight of the Backend Court
- **PRISMATICUS**, Keeper of the Database Scrolls
- **TRAEFIKUS**, Master of Load Balancing Arts
- **CLOUDFLARIUS**, Guardian of the Tunnel Gates
- **AZURIUS**, Keeper of the Storage Vaults
- **GOOGLIUS**, Oracle of Authentication
- **TYPESCRIPTUS**, Sage of Code Certainty
- **JESTUS**, Court Jester and Tester of Truth
- **ZODIUS**, Guardian of Validation
- **PINOCHIO**, Chronicle Keeper of Logs

---

## Act I: The Gathering of Technologies

### Scene I
*Enter REACTIA, with her court of Vite, shadcn, and Tailwind*

**REACTIA:**
Hark! What noble quest doth bring us here today,
To build a realm where containers hold sway?
With Vite's swift winds and Tailwind's graceful art,
We'll craft a dashboard to win every heart!

Mine UI shall shimmer with shadcn's design,
While Router DOM shall make the pathways shine.
React Query shall fetch data without cease,
And bring to users a most wondrous peace.

*Enter EXPRESSIO, bearing the banner of Node.js*

**EXPRESSIO:**
Fair maiden of the Frontend, hear me well!
I come bearing backend tales to tell.
With Express.js Five, my kingdom strong,
I'll serve thy needs both swift and long!

My SQLite chambers store what must be kept,
While Prisma guards the data while systems slept.
Authentication flows through Passport's gate,
Where Google's Oracle decides each fate.

### Scene II
*DOCKERUS appears in a cloud of containerized mist*

**DOCKERUS:**
Behold! I am the master of all realms virtual,
Where processes dance in chambers mystical!
Each container bears its sacred name and port,
While volumes mount like treasures in my court.

Mine API speaks through dockerode's tongue,
And status badges show if processes are young.
With real-time polling every five seconds hence,
I'll keep thy dashboard current and intense!

**TYPESCRIPTUS:**
(*entering with scrolls of type definitions*)
Fear not the chaos of untyped discourse!
I bring certainty to every source.
My shared types package, `@mini-infra/types`,
Shall prevent the errors that cause such gripes!

From client to server, my definitions flow,
Ensuring that all components truly know
What data structures they may expect,
And compile-time safety they'll never neglect!

---

## Act II: The Authentication Chronicles

### Scene I
*GOOGLIUS descends from the OAuth clouds*

**GOOGLIUS:**
Mortal developers, hear my sacred call!
I guard the gateway that protects you all.
With OAuth 2.0's most noble creed,
I verify each user's righteous deed.

Sessions persist in database halls,
While cross-tab sync answers duty's calls.
Protected routes shall guard thy precious views,
And public paths welcome all who peruse.

**PRISMATICUS:**
(*emerging from database caverns*)
Within my SQLite chambers deep and wide,
User profiles and sessions do reside.
My schema defines the structure true,
Where authentication data passes through.

Generated clients speak my ancient tongue,
And migrations keep my structure young.
With database push and studio's sight,
I make data management pure delight!

### Scene II
*JESTUS leaps forth with testing tools*

**JESTUS:**
Ha! While others build with serious might,
I ensure their work performs just right!
With Jest and Testing Library's art,
I probe each function and component part.

Mock authentication, isolate each test,
CUID2 data ensures we're at our best.
Coverage reports shall tell the tale,
Of which brave codes will pass or fail!

---

## Act III: The Container Management Saga

### Scene I
*DOCKERUS holds court with his container subjects*

**DOCKERUS:**
Come forth, ye containers great and small,
Answer when thy lord doth call!
Status running, stopped, or paused,
Each state in badges is fairly caused.

Mine dashboard shows your ports and IP,
Creation timestamps for all to see.
Filtering searches through name and image,
While pagination turns each data page.

**REACTIA:**
My useContainers hook doth poll with care,
Every five seconds checking what is there.
React Query caches with wisdom true,
While exponential backoff sees us through.

Debounced searching waits three hundred mills,
Before sending forth its query thrills.
Fifty containers per page displayed,
In responsive design expertly made!

### Scene II
*TRAEFIKUS appears wielding load balancer scales*

**TRAEFIKUS:**
Fear not the traffic's crushing weight!
I stand as guardian at the gate.
Blue-green deployments smooth and clean,
The finest load balancing ever seen!

Zero-downtime is my solemn vow,
Health checks ensure systems work somehow.
Container routing with precision true,
Load balanced paths for me and you!

---

## Act IV: The Storage and Tunneling Quest

### Scene I
*AZURIUS emerges from cloud storage realms*

**AZURIUS:**
From Azure's vast and boundless sky,
I bring storage services that never die!
PostgreSQL backups safe and sound,
In my blob containers can be found.

Connection strings with encryption blessed,
Keep secrets safe and data addressed.
Manual backups or cron's sweet song,
I'll keep thy databases safe and strong!

**CLOUDFLARIUS:**
(*descending through tunnel portals*)
Through tunnels dark and passages hidden,
I connect what was once forbidden!
API tokens guard my sacred trust,
While tunnel monitoring is a must.

Read-only views of connection state,
Real-time updates never late.
Account IDs and tunnel names,
All protected in security's flames!

### Scene II
*PINOCHIO enters with structured logs*

**PINOCHIO:**
I chronicle all that comes to pass,
With structured logging, none surpass!
High-performance and correlation true,
Request IDs shall see thee through.

Sensitive data redacted well,
No secrets in my logs to tell.
Error handling with context clear,
Makes debugging crystal clear!

---

## Act V: The Testing and Deployment Finale

### Scene I
*ZODIUS appears with validation schemas*

**ZODIUS:**
Runtime validation is my sacred art,
Type checking each request part!
Client and server both must pass,
My schema rules, a rigid class.

Malformed data shall not proceed,
When validation plants its seed.
Error responses clear and bright,
Guide users toward the path of right!

### Scene II
*All characters gather for the grand finale*

**TYPESCRIPTUS:**
Now gather 'round, ye noble crew,
Our workspace architecture is nearly through!
Three packages dance in harmony:
Client, server, and lib's decree!

**EXPRESSIO:**
With `npm run dev` we start our quest,
All services running at their best!
Watch mode keeps our builds in sync,
While hot reload quick as a wink!

**REACTIA:**
The dashboard gleams with data bright,
Container status in plain sight.
Real-time updates every second five,
Keep our infrastructure alive!

**DOCKERUS:**
My containers dance in orchestrated play,
Status badges show the way.
Filtering, sorting, pagination true,
A management system through and through!

**ALL:**
(*in unison*)
Thus ends our tale of Mini Infra's might,
Where containers dance and data takes flight!
A single Docker host to rule them all,
With PostgreSQL backups standing tall!

Zero-downtime deployments smooth and clean,
The finest infrastructure ever seen!
Authentication flows with Google's grace,
While real-time monitoring keeps the pace!

*Exeunt all, as the curtain falls on the containerized kingdom*
